Our Culture

Relentless pursuit of self improvement.

At Spire, we're constantly questioning our assumptions. How do we think creatively about packing sensors into satellites? About company outings? About data and analytics? It's not enough to accept the status quo. We look for improvement in everything. The first thing we did was throw away performance reviews. At Spire, every employee receives quarterly career coaching instead. We think that you should work on things that you love and we do our best to make sure that your workload is aligned with your own interests. We are collaborative to the core because we know that teams are greater than the sum of their parts. We work hard, move fast, and create amazing results.

FAQs

Our process shouldn't be a mystery. If you
have any additional questions, feel free to ask.

How soon after applying will I receive my challenge question?

The challenge will be sent automatically as soon as you apply. We move fast, and you should never be left waiting on us.

How often do you review challenges?

We review challenges twice per week, so you'll never have to wait more than a few days to hear back from us.

Under what circumstances can I skip a challenge?

Using challenges allows us to compare your answers to a standard criteria. We ask that all applicants complete the challenge for the position they apply for, though we are willing to accept code (or relevant work samples for non-developer positions) that exemplify the quality of your work. Depending upon the work samples provided, we may still ask you to complete the challenge. This is only to verify how you would approach certain scenarios that are common to the role for which you are applying.

What should I expect in the first phone screen?

We like to have a casual conversation with you to answer any questions that you have about Spire or the position and to get an initial gauge of how your expertise and personality might fit into our unique environment. These calls are meant to be brief, usually lasting about 30 minutes.

What should I expect in a technical phone screen?

Expect a call of about 45 minutes that focuses on your technical abilities. You can anticipate scenario-based questions which will help us get a feel for how you would solve challenges we’re working on right now, as well as some questions about your past work challenges. Developers can expect relevant mock coding questions.

How long is an in-person interview? What should I expect?

In-person interviews give you a chance to meet the team and experience our work environment first-hand. They normally last a half day, though we do ask you to prepare for a full day with us. Lunch provided, of course. Every candidate will also get to talk with a member of our executive team at this stage. You are encouraged to bring questions of your own; it is wonderful to learn more about how you are thinking about what we do.

How long will it be before I receive a response after an in-person interview?

You can expect a response no more than 2 business days after your in-person interview.

What if I don't live close to one of the Spire offices?

Don't worry about it! We'll take care of the travel arrangements for your in-person interview at Spire.
